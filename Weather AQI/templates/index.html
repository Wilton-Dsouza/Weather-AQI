About


AQI is a website which is used to give Air quality Index.

It takes the city name has the Input and it returns the various parameters related to Air quality and also shows the whether city has high level of air pollution or not.

The AQI is user friendly and There is no registering or sign in involved.

The website also has an Integrated maps in it for enchanced experience.
